## Etsy setup notes

Use the most specific planner or digital-planner category Etsy currently offers, then fill every relevant attribute that accurately applies.

Avoid:

- keyword-stuffed titles
- repeated gift phrases
- subjective filler words doing the work of concrete descriptors
- vague imagery that makes the product feel abstract or confusing

Prefer:

- a clear item name early in the title
- concrete descriptors tied to what the buyer is actually getting
- attributes filled in accurately
- image slides that explain product, delivery, and outcome in seconds

## Notes from current Etsy guidance

These points are based on Etsy Seller Handbook articles reviewed on April 23, 2026:

- Etsy says titles should be clear and easy to scan, with the item stated clearly and the most important traits up front
- Etsy says titles no longer need to carry the full search burden because search considers titles, tags, attributes, descriptions, first photo, and reviews together
- Etsy recommends avoiding repeated words and moving subjective or gift-heavy phrasing out of the title
- Etsy says accurate attributes help listings appear in relevant filtered results and additional browse surfaces
- Etsy recommends a mix of image types that communicate product clarity, context, scale, detail, packaging, and process
